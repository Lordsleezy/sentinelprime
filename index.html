What Is SentinelAI

Meet Sentinel - Your Personal AI Operating System

SentinelAI is a local-first AI assistant and automation platform that runs entirely on your own hardware. It gives you a personal AI operating system without renting your life back from a cloud subscription.

You talk to Sentinel through an orb interface. Ask for what you need, and the system routes your request to the right worker automatically: search, code, repair, organize, monitor, summarize, automate, or build something new.

Sentinel can control your smart home, manage your calendar, track your finances, trade markets in paper mode, write and repair code, monitor your health data, capture memory and notes, and more.

Everything runs on your machine. No subscriptions. No cloud requirement. Your data never leaves unless you explicitly connect an integration that needs the internet.
