FAQ

Do I need an account?
Yes — a free Sentinel account for your license key. No payment info required for the free tier.
Can I use my own Claude API key?
Yes — that's the Pro tier. You pay Anthropic directly and bring your own key.
What's the difference between Pro and Managed?
Pro: you pay Anthropic directly with BYOK. Managed: we handle the API, offer higher limits, and priority support.
Does Forge work without internet?
Yes — the free tier uses Ollama locally and works fully offline.
