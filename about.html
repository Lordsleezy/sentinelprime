Built by one person. Designed for everyone.

Sentinel is a bet that intelligence should not require surveillance. We ship privacy-first AI with no venture mandate to monetize your attention, no incentive to hoard your conversations, and no backroom deals with advertisers. The mission is simple: put powerful models and security tooling on devices people already own, keep the stack transparent and auditable, and refuse the extraction economy that defines big tech today.

Why Sentinel exists

The dominant playbook — centralized inference, opaque training, and default data collection — trains users to surrender context in exchange for convenience. Sentinel exists to break that pattern: local-first execution, explicit consent, and products you can audit.

What big tech gets wrong

When AI lives only in the cloud, every prompt becomes a dataset. When business models depend on engagement, privacy becomes a marketing line instead of an architecture. Sentinel answers with hardware-bound intelligence, minimal telemetry by design, and pricing that does not depend on reselling your life.

Values

Privacy · Transparency · Accessibility · Security

Those words are constraints on the roadmap, not poster copy. If a feature cannot be explained plainly, it does not ship.
