Why Sentinel exists

The dominant playbook — centralized inference, opaque training, and default data collection — trains users to surrender context in exchange for convenience. Sentinel exists to break that pattern: local-first execution, explicit consent, and products you can audit.

What big tech gets wrong

When AI lives only in the cloud, every prompt becomes a dataset. When business models depend on engagement, privacy becomes a marketing line instead of an architecture. Sentinel answers with hardware-bound intelligence, minimal telemetry by design, and pricing that does not depend on reselling your life.
