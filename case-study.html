Case Study

Twin Rivers Fence

How a local fence company went from scattered marketing to a structured digital system that supported expansion and better lead handling.

Problem

Twin Rivers Fence delivered excellent work, but their digital setup could not support consistent growth across nearby markets.

They relied on referrals and occasional ads, with no scalable web structure or clean process for handling incoming inquiries.

Solution

Sentinel Prime designed and launched a multi-location web architecture with city-targeted pages and SEO fundamentals built for long-term visibility.

We also implemented a conversion-focused lead capture flow so inquiries were routed quickly and followed up consistently.
