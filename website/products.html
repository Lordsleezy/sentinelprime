SentinelAI is a local-first personal operating system built around an orb interface. Speak or type a request, and Sentinel routes it to the right worker automatically: automation, code, search, security, markets, memory, home control, or a new tool it builds for you.

How The Orb Works

One interface. Many workers.

The orb is the user-facing brain. It listens for intent, chooses the right worker, checks whether approval is needed, and gives the result back in plain language. OpenClaw handles commands, Forge writes and repairs code, Guardian scans and protects, Earn finds opportunities, Market tracks paper trading, Voice handles wake word flows, and the Home Assistant bridge connects your smart home.

Self-Extending

When a tool does not exist, Sentinel can build it.

SentinelAI can search for existing capability, route known work to existing workers, or ask for approval before Forge builds a new tool. This keeps automation useful without letting an AI silently expand itself behind your back.
