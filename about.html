Story

From repair bot to personal AI OS.

Sentinel Prime is founded by Paul Green, building from San Jose, CA. The project started as a repair bot: an assistant that could diagnose broken software, fix code, and keep working without needing constant babysitting.

That repair loop grew into a larger idea: a full life assistant that runs locally, routes work to specialized agents, respects approval gates, and helps people automate their real lives without handing everything to a subscription cloud.

Sentinel Prime is pre-incorporation and filing as Sentinel Prime Inc.

Mission

Private. Local. Yours.

SentinelAI is built around local-first execution, privacy by default, open architecture, no subscriptions, and user ownership. The goal is not another chatbot tab. The goal is a personal operating layer that belongs to the person using it.

Values
